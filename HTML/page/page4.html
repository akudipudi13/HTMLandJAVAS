CHIPOTLE BURRITO

    Protein:
      1.5 lbs chicken thighs
      2 tbsp chipotle peppers
      1 tbsp chili powder
      2 cloves garlic (minced)
      Oil, salt, pepper
    Rice
      2 cups white rice
      1/4 cup cilantro
      1 lime (juiced)
    Fillings
      Flour tortillas
      1 can black beans
      1 green bell pepper
      1 diced red onion
    Toppings
      Monterey Jack cheese
      Sour cream
      1-2 avacados
      salsa
      Romain Lettuce


    Prep Chicken
      Mix the chopped chipotle peppers, chili powder, and garlic. Rub on chicken and cook in a skillet until browned. Dice it up.
    Prep Rice
      Stir the lime juice and cilantro into your warm, cooked rice.
    Saute Veggies
      Cook the peppers and onions in a pan with a little oil until soft.
    Assemble
      Warm the tortilla, pile in the rice, beans, chicken, and toppings. Fold the sides in and roll it tight.
